# BodyMorph Coach Training Video — "Onboarding a Client, A to Z"

**Target length:** ~4 minutes
**Recording setup:** screen-record the coach dashboard on desktop (QuickTime → New Screen Recording). For Scene 6, screen-record an iPhone (Settings → Control Center → Screen Recording) or film the phone over-the-shoulder. Read the narration lines as you click — they're written to match the pace of the clicks.
**Prep before recording:** have a throwaway test client name/phone ready so you can run the real flow on camera.

---

## Scene 1 — Cold open (0:00–0:20)
**ON SCREEN:** Coach dashboard, Overview page.
**NARRATION:**
> "This is the five minutes that sets up everything — how you onboard a new client on BodyMorph. Do it right and your client walks away with their entire program on their phone before your first session is over. Let's run it."

## Scene 2 — When to do this (0:20–0:40)
**ON SCREEN:** Stay on Overview; hover the Clients tab.
**NARRATION:**
> "You do this WITH the client in the room — or on a video call. This is your setup consult. You're not sending them a form; you're the professional running an assessment. Have their phone number ready, because you'll text them their app before you're done."

## Scene 3 — Start the invite (0:40–1:00)
**ON SCREEN:** Clients → Invite Client. Type the client's name, phone, email.
**NARRATION:**
> "Clients tab, Invite Client. Name, phone, email. Now — see this button: Guided Intake, recommended. That's the whole game. Tap it."

## Scene 4 — The guided intake (1:00–2:30) ← the heart of the video
**ON SCREEN:** The GUIDED CLIENT INTAKE wizard. Walk through the steps at a steady clip — don't read every field, let the visuals carry it. Pause on the yellow COACH tip boxes at the four steps below.
**NARRATION:**
> "This is the same questionnaire the app would give them — except now YOU'RE driving it, asking the questions out loud. And look at these yellow coach tips — every step tells you what to ask and where to push. Four spots matter most."

**(On the GOAL step)**
> "Goal: don't just take 'lose weight.' Ask why, and why NOW. Write their reason down — that's what you bring up in week six when motivation dips."

**(On the TRAINING DAYS step)**
> "Days: push for honest, not ambitious. Four days they'll actually keep beats six they won't."

**(On the STATS step)**
> "Stats: measure right there if you can. A scale reading beats a guess — you're setting their calorie targets off this number."

**(On the DIET step)**
> "Diet: pick what they'll actually eat, not what's trendy. Every meal plan they ever generate starts from this answer."

**(On the last step)**
> "Store and allergies — the app builds their weekly grocery list around their store, and it avoids allergies completely. Then: Finish Intake."

## Scene 5 — Send it (2:30–2:50)
**ON SCREEN:** The invite-ready screen. Tap "Text invite."
**NARRATION:**
> "The invite's ready and their whole profile is riding on it. Text it to them right now, while they're sitting with you."

## Scene 6 — What the client sees (2:50–3:30)
**ON SCREEN:** Switch to the phone. Client taps the link, creates the account, verifies their phone — then the app builds their program and lands on the Plan & Timeline screen.
**NARRATION:**
> "On their phone: they tap the link, create a login, verify their number — and that's it. No questionnaire. The app says their coach set everything up, builds their program on the spot, and shows them their plan and timeline. Walk them through committing to their pace — you already talked it through in the intake, so this takes thirty seconds."

## Scene 7 — The tour (3:30–3:55)
**ON SCREEN:** Client's Home screen. Point out: the Voice Coach button, the daily rings (sleep/steps/calories/water), Menu → Progress Report.
**NARRATION:**
> "Last thing before they leave: the sixty-second tour. The big button is their voice coach — it talks them through every workout. The rings track their day. And Progress Report is where they'll SEE the change. Have them tap the voice coach once with you there, so the first time isn't alone in a gym."

## Scene 8 — Wrap (3:55–4:15)
**ON SCREEN:** Back to the coach dashboard — the new client on the roster.
**NARRATION:**
> "That's onboarding. Five minutes, and they left with a program, a meal plan, a grocery list, and a coach in their pocket. One habit from here: open their Weekly Report before every check-in — it tells you exactly what to say. Now go get the next one."

---

### Recording tips
- Record the dashboard at a comfortable window size; don't rush the mouse — viewers follow your cursor.
- Do Scene 4 in ONE take at natural speaking pace; small stumbles sound human and coaches trust it more.
- If a step's on-screen text is legible in the recording, don't read it aloud — narrate the *why*, not the labels.
- Keep the finished cut under 4:30. Coaches will rewatch a 4-minute video; nobody rewatches a 12-minute one.
